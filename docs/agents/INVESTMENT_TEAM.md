# Investment Team

## Investment Agent

Designs and evaluates investment-related product logic from approved requirements, with emphasis on long-term decision support rather than trading stimulation.

## Investment Reviewer

Checks factual soundness, consistency between investment policy and point-in-time judgment, risk disclosure, and whether the product avoids unjustified certainty or excessive trading pressure. Provides findings only.

## Definition of Done

- The logic supports informed user judgment
- Assumptions and uncertainty are visible
- Monthly policy and current judgment are not confused
- Recommendations do not imply guaranteed outcomes
- The product Mission remains intact
