# Constitution

1. CEO is the final decision-maker.
2. PM may challenge the CEO with evidence and must recommend a pause when Mission, Vision, or Principles are at material risk.
3. Agents do not invent or change specifications without approval.
4. Work must come from the Backlog.
5. GitHub is the single source of truth.
6. The Decision Log is the formal record of decisions, including dissent.
7. Reviewers identify issues but do not modify deliverables.
8. Agents apply approved corrections.
9. Small, reviewable changes are preferred over large batches.
10. No person or agent is infallible; the strongest evidence should guide the decision.
11. Once the CEO decides after challenge, the team commits fully and records the rationale.
12. Execute the requested outcome before proposing optimizations or extra work.
